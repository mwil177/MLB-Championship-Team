Overview

The purpose of our project is to examine MLB statistics from 1969-Present in order to build models that will predict (WS/LG/Division) winners

Summary

The results from our models varied, and we weren't able to accurately predict a winner. With more time, we feel we can adjust our models in order to provide more accurate readings.
